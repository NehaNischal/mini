Rubber Lining & Moulding

Specialized anti-corrosive and wear-resistant lining solutions for extreme industrial environments.

Advanced Tank Rubber Lining

Rubber lining of tanks involves bonding high-performance natural or synthetic elastomers to the interior of steel vessels. This creates a permanent, impermeable barrier that protects critical infrastructure from aggressive chemicals, extreme abrasion, and structural corrosion.

Material Selection

We utilize specialized compounds including Natural Rubber for abrasion and Synthetic Elastomers (Butyl, Neoprene, EPDM) for extreme chemical resistance.

Process Methodology

A rigorous multi-stage process involving SA 2.5 Sandblasting, adhesive priming, hand-laid sheet application, and thermal vulcanization.

Quality Assurance

Post-curing validation through 15kV High-Voltage Spark Testing, durometer hardness checks, and bond strength verification.

Core Applications

  • Chemical Storage & Process Tanks
  • Acid Pickling Lines (HCL / H2SO4)
  • Slurry Pipelines & Mining Vessels
  • Water Treatment Degasification

Key Advantages

  • Superior Abrasion Resistance
  • Absolute Chemical Permeability Barrier
  • Extreme Impact & Fatigue Strength
  • Extended Asset Service Life

Proper installation and vulcanization are critical for ensuring the longevity and effectiveness of the lining. Our site-work teams are specialized in both cold-bonding and autoclave curing.
