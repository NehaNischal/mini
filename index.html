Common Questions

Industrial Engineering FAQ

What is industrial rubber lining?

Industrial rubber lining is a specialized process of applying rubber sheets to the interior of tanks, vessels, and pipes to provide chemical resistance and prevent corrosion.

Why is FRP lining used?

FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) lining is used for its extreme durability and resistance to highly corrosive chemicals, making it ideal for industrial floorings and chemical storage tanks.

What are the benefits of sand blasting?

Sand blasting provides a superior surface preparation method for applying protective coatings, ensuring maximum adhesion and longevity of the industrial coating.
