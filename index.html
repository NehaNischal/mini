OUR LEGACY

Company Overview

Mini Enterprises, established in 1977, is a professionally managed engineering company run by highly qualified professionals with over 45 years of proven experience in executing specialized engineering works. Since our inception, we have successfully undertaken a wide range of contract works, mainly serving the public sector and major industrial organizations with reliability and technical excellence. Today, we proudly serve major undertakings including FACT, ISRO, IREL, TCC, BPCL COCHIN REFINERY, MALABAR CEMENTS, KSINC etc and many other reputed organizations with a strong commitment to quality and professional excellence.
