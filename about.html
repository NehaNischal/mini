Company Overview

Legacy of 45+ Years

Experts in Industrial Engineering

Mini Enterprises, established in 1977, is a professionally managed engineering company run by highly qualified professionals with over 45 years of proven experience in executing specialized engineering works. Since our inception, we have successfully undertaken a wide range of contract works, mainly serving the public sector and major industrial organizations with reliability and technical excellence.

We undertake a wide range of engineering and specialized activities and are fully equipped with modern equipment required for blasting, rubber lining, and other specialized operations. With our own in-house manufacturing unit, we maintain strict quality standards and ensure dependable project execution. Today, in 2026, we proudly serve major Central and State Government undertakings and leading industries, including FACT Ltd, VSSC/ISRO, IREL Ltd, TCC Ltd, refineries, and many other reputed organizations. With decades of expertise and a strong commitment to quality, safety, and professional excellence, MINI ENTERPRISES continues to deliver trusted engineering services that meet the highest industry standards.
